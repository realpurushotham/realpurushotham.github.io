About me

Designing with
purpose & empathy

I've spent over 8 years at the intersection of design and product thinking — working across startups, scale-ups, and enterprise teams to ship experiences that are both beautiful and genuinely useful.

My process is rooted in understanding people first. I believe great design isn't just how something looks — it's how it makes people feel and how effortlessly it gets out of the way.

Outside of screens, I'm drawn to nature, stories, and anything that makes me think differently about the world.
